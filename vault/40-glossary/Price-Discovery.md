# Price Discovery

The process by which a market **arrives at a price** through the interaction of buyers and sellers — not by decree. Every new order, cancel, and trade updates the [[Bid-Ask-Spread]] and therefore the "price" everyone observes (usually the mid-point, or the last trade).

Key insight: **the price is an output, not an input.** In Agora, no one sets the price of the instrument. Traders submit orders; the matching engine (Milestone 5) resolves them; the top of the book *is* the current price. Watching a coherent price series emerge from random-looking order flow is the payoff of the whole project (Milestone 7).
