# Bid-Ask Spread

- **Bid** — the highest price a buyer is currently willing to pay. (Best bid = top of the buy side.)
- **Ask (offer)** — the lowest price a seller is currently willing to accept. (Best ask = top of the sell side.)
- **Spread** — `ask − bid`. The gap between them.

The spread is the [[Market-Maker]]'s pay for providing [[Liquidity]], and a direct measure of market quality: **tight spread = liquid, healthy; wide spread = thin, uncertain, or volatile.** Watching the spread widen is an early sign of stress (Milestone 7).

A trade only happens when someone crosses the spread — an aggressive buyer paying the ask, or an aggressive seller hitting the bid. See [[Passive-vs-Aggressive]] and [[Price-Discovery]].
