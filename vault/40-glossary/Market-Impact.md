# Market Impact

The amount your **own order moves the price against you**. Buy aggressively and you eat through resting asks from cheapest upward — each unit costs more than the last. That adverse price movement is market impact.

- Small (retail) orders: negligible impact.
- Large (institutional) orders: large impact → why big players **slice** one big order into many small ones over time (execution algorithms like TWAP/VWAP).
- Directly tied to [[Liquidity]]: deep book → low impact; thin book → high impact.

Agora lets you *measure* this: send a large market order and watch how far the price walks (Milestone 7–8). Related: [[Price-Discovery]], [[Bid-Ask-Spread]].
